CleanFeedFB

Hide Facebook posts with Follow and Join buttons

About

The CleanFeedFB extension automatically detects and hides posts in your Facebook feed that contain "Follow" or "Join" buttons, helping you maintain a cleaner, more focused feed experience.

Key Features:

  • Automatic Detection: Scans for posts containing Follow or Join buttons
  • Real-time Filtering: Works as new content loads in your feed
  • Toggle Control: Easily enable/disable the extension
  • Daily Statistics: Track how many posts are filtered each day
  • Lightweight: Minimal performance impact on your browsing

Privacy: This extension works entirely locally in your browser. No data is collected or sent to external servers.
